The problem

Mid-market businesses know AI works. They can't deploy it.

Every operating business above $1M ARR has the same internal conversation: "We should be using AI." The conversation never ends in a deployment because the available paths are all bad.

1
Build it themselves.

Hire engineers. 6–18 months. High burn, high failure rate. They aren't software companies and don't want to be.

2
Hire a consultancy.

Strategy decks, roadmap PDFs, pilots that never ship. Revenue model is decoupled from outcomes. Most engagements end without working software.

3
Buy point tools.

An AI for sales, an AI for support, an AI for ops. Now they have 8 dashboards no one logs into and no integration with the business.
