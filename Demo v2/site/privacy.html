This policy describes how Ramped AI ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website at 30dayramp.com or engage us as a customer.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you give us directly — your name, email, company, role, and the answers you submit on forms (booking, questionnaire, free-roadmap). For active customers, we additionally process operational data inside the systems we integrate (HubSpot, NetSuite, etc.) under the scope of the engagement.

We also collect basic usage data automatically: pages viewed, referring URL, device type, and IP-derived approximate location. We do not buy or rent third-party data about you.

2. How we use it

  • To respond to inquiries and run discovery calls
  • To deliver the deployment work you've engaged us for
  • To send transactional email (calendar invites, deliverables)
  • To improve our website and offerings — in aggregate, never identified

We do not sell your information. We do not use customer operational data to train any third-party AI model.

3. Who we share it with

We use a small set of vendors to run the business. Each receives only what they need:

  • Vercel — hosting
  • Supabase — application database
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Google — calendar & meeting
  • Anthropic — LLM provider for our deployed agents (with no-train-on-data terms)

We share customer data with these processors under written DPAs. We do not share with anyone else except where required by law.

4. Cookies & tracking

We use a single first-party analytics cookie to count visits and basic usage. We do not run third-party ad pixels. We honor "Do Not Track" headers when sent.

5. Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing jon@30dayramp.com. We typically respond within 7 business days.

If you're in the EU, UK, or California, you have additional rights under GDPR / CCPA — including the right to data portability and the right to object to processing. Same email applies.

6. Security

Customer data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). Access is least-privilege and audited. We use SSO + MFA for all internal tools. We carry cyber liability insurance and run quarterly access reviews.

If we ever experience a breach affecting your data, we'll notify you within 72 hours of confirmation.
