## #6 — Controversy / contrarian

Enterprise tools (UiPath, Automation Anywhere) are sold to *IT departments*. But IT departments don't understand the business workflows.

The right buyer for AI automation in a $5M-$50M business is a CEO who wants someone else to own the outcome.
