## How to use

1. **Pick the channel** you're working in
2. **Open the file** for that channel
3. **Customize** the templates with your specific situation
4. **Track what works** — when a post performs, save the variant; when it doesn't, kill it

## The voice in 5 rules

1. **Numbers over adjectives.** "Save 40+ hours/week" beats "save tons of time"
2. **Operator, not vendor.** Write like the CEO talking to another CEO
3. **No fluff.** Cut "we believe", "we're passionate about", "we're dedicated to"
4. **Show, don't tell.** "Slack message → bot replies in 14s" beats "fast response times"
5. **One idea per post.** Don't try to say everything

## When to update this pack

- After a customer win → add to case studies
- After a sales call → add the objection that came up
- After a post performs → save the variant
- After a competitor pivots → revise positioning lines
