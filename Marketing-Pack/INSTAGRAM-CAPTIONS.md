## Hashtag strategy

Don't use more than 5 hashtags per post. Saturated tags hurt reach. Use a mix of:

**Niche (4-5 of these):**
- #aiagents
- #automation
- #b2bsaas
- #operations
- #smallbusiness
- #founder
- #startuplife

**Avoid:**
- #ai (too saturated)
- #artificialintelligence (too generic)
- #productivity (low buyer intent)
- #motivation (wrong audience)

## Posting cadence

| Frequency | Type | Why |
|---|---|---|
| 3x / week | Static post | Steady feed presence |
| 1x / week | Carousel | Saves and shares (algorithm boost) |
| 2-3x / week | Reels | Reach amplification |
| Daily | Stories | Founder-presence + behind-the-scenes |

Best post times for B2B founder audience:
- Tuesday 8-10am ET
- Wednesday 11am-1pm ET
- Thursday 7-9pm ET (late catchup)

---

## What NOT to post on Instagram

- Stock photos labeled "Future of work"
- Inspirational quotes overlaid on sunsets
- Anything with "Manifest", "Hustle", or "Mindset"
- Multiple CTAs in one post
- More than 1 paragraph of body copy on a non-carousel
- Memes from other industries that don't translate
- Customer logos without permission
