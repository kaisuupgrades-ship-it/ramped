## FAQ updates

### Current FAQs (assumed):
- What does Ramped do?
- How is this different from Lindy/Gumloop/Zapier?
- What's included in each tier?
- What's the 30-day guarantee?

### Variants to add / test:

**"What if my industry is unusual?"**
> "If your industry has CRM/email/Slack/accounting, we work with it. We've deployed in dental, logistics, manufacturing, property management, professional services, and DTC. The agents are configured per-customer, so industry specificity comes from setup, not the underlying tech."

**"How do you handle data privacy?"**
> "Your data stays in your tools. We don't store customer data — agents read from and write to your existing systems via API. SOC 2 Type II underway, full security audit available on request for Enterprise."

**"What if my team doesn't trust AI?"**
> "Approval-by-default — every customer-facing action goes through your team's one-click approval before sending. Most teams move from 'I have to approve everything' (week 1) to 'auto-send the trusted patterns' (month 3) on their own timeline."

**"What happens if Ramped goes out of business?"**
> "You own the agent configurations and the data. We'd hand off documentation + runbooks for self-hosting. We're a 4-person team backed by [investors]. Stable but small — ask for our latest financials if it matters to your contract."

## Test plan (12-week roadmap)

| Week | Test | Hypothesis |
|---|---|---|
| 1-2 | Hero h1: current vs Variant A (outcome-forward) | Specific outcomes convert better |
| 3-4 | Hero subhead: current vs Variant A (concrete tools) | Mentioning Slack/HubSpot raises CTR |
| 5-6 | CTA: current vs "Talk to Andrew" | Founder-led trust converts higher |
| 7-8 | Numbers section: current vs Variant A (vertical-specific) | Vertical specificity beats generic |
| 9-10 | Differentiator: current vs Variant A (comparison table) | Tables convert when comparing to competitors |
| 11-12 | Pricing: current vs Variant A (guarantee-forward) | Risk-reversal beats feature lists |

Track at each step:
- Hero CTR (clicks on primary CTA / sessions)
- Discovery call booking rate
- Time on page
- Scroll depth

---

## What NOT to test

- Wholesale page redesigns (too many variables, can't isolate cause)
- Pricing increases without a value-add change
- Removing the 30-day guarantee (it's load-bearing for trust)
- Replacing "Andrew Yoon" with a generic "Our team" (founder credibility is part of why we close)
