## FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

# Ramped AI Launches "Done-for-You AI Department" Service for Operating Businesses

## New B2B service deploys and operates AI agents inside customer Slack, HubSpot, and other ops stacks on a flat monthly retainer. 30-day go-live guarantee.

**LOS ANGELES — April 30, 2026** — Ramped AI today announced the public launch of its done-for-you AI implementation service, designed for operating businesses with $5M-$50M in annual revenue who want AI automation but don't have the in-house capacity to build and maintain it.

Unlike DIY AI tools (such as Lindy, Gumloop, or Zapier AI) that require operators to build and maintain agents themselves, Ramped deploys a complete suite of AI agents inside the customer's existing software stack — Slack, HubSpot, NetSuite, QuickBooks, Gmail — and operates the agents on an ongoing monthly retainer. Customers approve every customer-facing action; Ramped owns implementation and uptime.

The company's signature 30-day go-live guarantee promises a working AI agent in production within 30 days of contract signing. If Ramped fails to meet the deadline, the entire onboarding fee is refunded.

"We're not selling software," said Andrew Yoon, founder and CEO of Ramped AI. "We're selling a department. Same operating model as your bookkeeper or your fractional CFO — except instead of accounting, we run your AI agents. Most operators don't have the time or technical skill to maintain AI tools themselves. They need someone else to own the outcome."

### A new operating model for AI in mid-market

The mid-market AI implementation gap has been widely reported. Forrester estimates 67% of AI initiatives at companies under $50M in revenue are abandoned within 12 months due to maintenance overhead. Ramped's pricing — $2,500/mo Starter, $5,000/mo Growth — is designed to align with the cost of one part-time operations VA, with the goal of replacing 2-4 such hires per customer.

"We deployed Ramped at our 4-location dental group last month," said an early Ramped customer. "Three weeks in, the agents were already handling patient confirmations and insurance pre-auth follow-ups that used to take 14 hours of staff time per week. Same monthly cost as the part-time hire we were going to make. Better output. Doesn't quit."

### Founder background

Ramped AI was founded by Andrew Yoon, a 10-year operator and founder of Xtractor Depot, a precision lab equipment supplier serving customers including SpaceX, Lucid Motors, and dozens of cannabis and pharmaceutical labs. Yoon experienced the pain of manual operations work firsthand at Xtractor Depot, where the company employed four operations virtual assistants doing repetitive workflows totaling roughly $14,400/month in fully-loaded cost.

"We tried every tool. We tried more VAs. We tried consultants," Yoon said. "Eventually I built the AI department I wished I'd had. Once it was running at Xtractor Depot, friends in adjacent industries asked us to build it for them too. That's how Ramped became its own company."

### About Ramped AI

Ramped AI deploys AI agents inside operating businesses on a flat monthly retainer. Live in 30 days, or it's free. The company is headquartered in Los Angeles and serves customers across logistics, dental, manufacturing, professional services, and DTC e-commerce. ## Talking points for follow-up interviews

When journalists call after seeing the release, lead with these:

**On differentiation:**
"Most AI companies sell software. We sell a department. The difference is who owns the outcome when something breaks."

**On the 30-day guarantee:**
"30 days isn't aspirational. It's a forcing function. If we can't ship a working agent in 30 days, the scope was wrong or we were undisciplined. Either way, the customer shouldn't pay for that."

**On AI hype:**
"AI is real. The implementation problem is real. We're not building general AI — we're solving the operations bottleneck for businesses that can't hire AI engineers but need AI to actually work."

**On the future:**
"Best-case Ramped is the AT&T of mid-market AI departments — a critical operating partner for thousands of businesses. That's a 20-year company, not a 5-year flip."

**On hiring:**
"We're a 4-person team today. Hiring senior implementation managers — operators with 10+ years of mid-market business experience who want to learn AI tooling. Strong opinion: AI operators of the future come from operations, not from engineering."
